Burger



Description


Delicious yummy cheeseburger

Looks like a cheeseburger that you need to spend quite a lot of time looking for


Ingredients

  • 2 Buns
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Get 2 buns
  2. Put chopped tomato and onions on top of one of the buns
  3. Place cheese over the tomato and onions
  4. Place meat over the cheese
  5. Close the burger with the other bun
